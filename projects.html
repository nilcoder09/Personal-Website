CultureVibe

CultureVibe is a social media app that allows users to share and explore cultural experiences through photo posts. Users can: Post Photos: Share photos of cultural events, traditions, food, attire, and more. Update whenever you want. Like, Comment, and Save: Engage with other users' posts by liking, commenting, and saving them to personal collections. Explore: Discover cultural experiences from around the world through the explore feature. Edit Profile: Customize their profiles with personal information and cultural background. Top Creater: Highlight users who engage most with content by reading and interacting.
